What is Q-ID?

Q-ID (Queue Identity) is an AI-powered, edge-deployed biometric system that converts ordinary cameras into intelligent identity sensors.

  • Detects real humans, not just images
  • Tracks presence inside premises spatiotemporally
  • Records attendance automatically upon entry/exit
  • No cards, no punch machines, no manual intervention
  • 100% local processing - Data never leaves your premises
